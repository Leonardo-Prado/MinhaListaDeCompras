Privacy Policy

Welcome to Empta - Minha Lista de compras! Empta - Minha Lista de compras is an Android application that helps you manage your shopping lists. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our application.

When you create an account on Empta - Minha Lista de compras, we collect the following information:

  • Username: Your chosen username is used to identify you within the application.
  • Password: Your password is securely stored and used to authenticate you when you log in to the application.
  • Shopping Lists: We store the shopping lists you create in the application. This includes the items you add to your lists and any notes or details you provide.

We use your personal information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Authentication: Your username and password are used to authenticate you when you log in to the application.
  • Personalization: We use the information you provide to personalize your experience within the application, such as displaying your shopping lists and preferences.
  • Communication: We may use your email address to communicate with you about your account or to send you updates and notifications related to the application.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you would like us to delete your account and personal information, please email us at xleosp@gmail.com. Please note that deleting your account will also delete all of your shopping lists and associated data.

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our handling of your personal information, please contact us at xleosp@gmail.com.
